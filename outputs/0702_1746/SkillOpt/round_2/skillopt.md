## Learned Rules

1. **Answer Concisely**: When answering questions, output only the most direct and concise answer. Prioritize words or short phrases, avoiding additional descriptions, modifiers, full titles, or expanded forms unless the question explicitly requests more detail.

2. **Identify Answer Type**: First, identify the type of answer required (e.g., work title, person's name, conceptual term). Then, output the corresponding entity directly, without confusing related elements or adding irrelevant details.
